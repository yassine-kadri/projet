The Importance of Effective Performance Evaluation Frameworks

A evaluation grid is a structured tool used to assess and rate various aspects of a product, project, performance, or skill. It typically consists of a list of specific criteria accompanied by levels of performance or scores assigned to each criterion. Evaluation grids are commonly used in educational, professional, and research settings to provide an objective and consistent assessment of performance.
